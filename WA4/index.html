Artist Statement

Pruett Maddux (b. 2003) is a Tennessee-based artist exploring mixed-media found-object sculpture.
Inspired by her upbringing in southern Appalachia, her work calls back to American folk traditions.
Pruett is currently pursuing a B.F.A in Sculpture and Post-Studio Practice at the University of Colorado-Boulder.
